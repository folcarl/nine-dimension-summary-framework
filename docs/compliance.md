# Compliance

The default workflow expects user-provided text, transcripts, or document content.

## Media And URLs

URL handling is optional and is only a convenience path for content the user has rights to process. The project should not be described as a media acquisition or downloader product.

Users are responsible for complying with copyright law, platform terms, privacy obligations, and local rules.

## Do Not Commit

- cookies;
- downloaded media;
- private transcripts;
- logs;
- generated job state;
- temporary files;
- credentials.

## Verification Claims

Do not claim external verification unless it was actually performed. If the summary is based only on a transcript, say so.

## Investment And Economic Material

Outputs are for judgment support and research framing. They are not investment advice.
