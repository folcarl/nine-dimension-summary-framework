# Lightweight Example: Article Summary

## Core Content

The synthetic article argues that productivity tools often fail because teams adopt them before clarifying decision rights, workflows, and feedback loops.

## Real Problem

On the surface, the article discusses software adoption. More deeply, it handles the problem of organizations using tools to avoid managerial decisions.

## Form / Viewing Risk

The piece is a short analytical essay. Its risk is that readers may overgeneralize from a clean conceptual argument and ignore differences between team size, regulation, and operational complexity.

## Main Limitation

The article offers no concrete case study or comparative evidence.

## Overall Evaluation And Scores

Overall judgment: useful as a framing note, but too thin to support operational decisions by itself.
